BEST CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

Description

With 11 million page views and counting since 2013, these super soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies are the most popular cookie recipe on my website. Melted butter, more brown sugar than white sugar, cornstarch, and an extra egg yolk guarantee the absolute chewiest chocolate chip cookie texture. And you dont even need a mixer!

There are thousands of chocolate chip cookies recipes out there. Everyone has their favorite and this one is mine.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • melted butter
  • chocolate chips
  • baking soda
  • vanila

Steps to create the best cookies

  1. Preheat the oven at 200 degrees
  2. Chill the dough
  3. Roll the cookie dough balls extra tall
  4. Bake for 13 minutes or until brownish
