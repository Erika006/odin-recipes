CHEESY MAC N CHEESE

Description

This baked mac and cheese is a family favorite recipe, loved by both children and adults. It uses a combination of cheeses, layered in the dish as well as melted into a rich and creamy cheese sauce, for the ultimate in cheesy deliciousness! Perfect for a comforting dinner or as a holiday side dish!

Its nearly the universal comfort food, and there are SO many variations!

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • variations of cheese
  • bacon
  • milk
  • eggs

Steps to create THE mac n cheese

  1. Boil the pasta, drain
  2. Make the cheese sauce by combining butter and a bit of flour
  3. Cook the cheese sauce
  4. Mix with the cooked pasta on low heat
